Crimson Connect

Crimson Connect is a student-led initiative that randomly matches Harvard students for small group coffee or lunch meetups, helping them break out of their social bubbles, foster meaningful connections, and appreciate the diversity within the Harvard community.

Privacy and Safety

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities. To ensure security:

  • Only Harvard students and affiliates with Harvard email addresses are eligible to sign up.
  • We ask for minimal data: your Harvard email and availability.
  • Your data will be used solely for meetup matching and scheduling purposes and will be deleted after one month.
