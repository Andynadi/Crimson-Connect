    About Us

    Crimson Meet encourages real-world connections in an increasingly digital world. Decades ago, people spent twice as much time meeting in person as they do now. With the rise of social media, we're more connected than ever, yet increasingly tied to our screens — often missing out on genuine, face-to-face interactions. Crimson Meet is a student-led pilot initiative designed to foster small-group, in-person meetups, helping students form meaningful connections beyond their usual circles.

    By randomly pairing students for small group meetups, we aim to create opportunities for unexpected friendships, discovering new inspirations, or building professional networks.

    Crimson Meet is NOT a dating platform!

    Data and Privacy

    We collect only university email address required for pairing students into small groups. We keep your email address for two months and then erase it completely. We do not share your information with anyone outside of the Crimson Meet team.
