Privacy Policy for Crimson Connect

Overview

Crimson Connect is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our meetup coordination service.

Information Collection Methods

We collect your information through:

  • Google Forms for initial user applications
  • Form responses stored in Google's secure infrastructure
  • Protected Google Sheets for temporary data storage
  • Data transfer to XYZ server for matching purposes

Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Harvard email address
  • School or program affiliation (optional)
  • Location preferences (Harvard Square or Cambridge)
  • Available days for meetups
  • Time availability preferences

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to:

  • Create meetup groups of 2-4 Harvard students within 48 hours
  • Send email notifications about matches
  • Provide location suggestions for meetups
  • Generate calendar invites for scheduled meetings

Data Storage and Security

Infrastructure

  • Initial data collection through Google Forms infrastructure
  • Primary processing and matching on XYZ server
  • Encrypted data transfer between systems

Data Management Protocol

  • Form responses are exported from Google Sheets every 48 hours
  • Data is securely transferred to XYZ server for matching
  • Original form responses are archived and removed from Google Sheets
  • Matching data is retained only for 48 hours after successful pairing

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Submit feedback about the service
  • Report concerns about other users
  • Request removal of your information
  • Opt out of future matchings

Safety Guidelines

  • All users must use valid Harvard email addresses
  • Matches are limited to current Harvard students
  • Meeting locations are restricted to Harvard Square and Cambridge areas
  • Users receive safety guidelines with each match

Contact Information

For any concerns or questions, contact us through this: crimsonconnect@gmail.com

By using Crimson Connect, you acknowledge and agree to these terms.
